About me ☺︎

Hello! My name is Kyla and I have a love for storytelling. I focus in storyboarding and character design, but I also enjoy animating. I'm often working on my personal animation and story projects in my free time.

Some series that have inspired me are Don't Hug Me I'm Scared, Spider-man: Across the Spider-verse, The Amazing World of Gumball, The Amazing Digital Circus, ENA Dream BBQ, Smile For Me, A Hat in Time, Popee the Performer, and Deltarune.

I am drawn towards wacky stories with bright colors and thought-provoking stories. I hope to one day create something similar to the things that inspired me!
